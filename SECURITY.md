# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue, please report it privately to avoid
premature disclosure. You can reach the security team at
oss@veridia.io.

When reporting, please include:

- A description of the vulnerability and its impact
- Steps to reproduce or a proof of concept
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or configuration details

## Disclosure Expectations

- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 5 business days.
- We will investigate and work to provide a remediation plan or fix.
- Please allow a reasonable amount of time for us to address the issue before
  making any public disclosure.
